My Top 3 Toplaners

Gnar

Gnar has 2 characters inside of him, ranged form called "Mini Gnar" and melee form called "Mega Gnar"
He doesn't use mana or energy but gathers rage, and that's what he uses to change, When he reaches 100 rage
he changes to Mega Gnar.
Mega Gnar: Tanky Melee form, with cc
Mini Gnar: Ranged form with low cc with quite a lot of range

Pros:

  • Ranged where majority are melee
  • Melee form gives him a lot of cc, while having a lot of Damage.
  • Stacks while in ranged mode, giving him extra Movement speed and
    extra damage dealt when having enough stacks
  • Strong early but also quite strong late game

Cons:

  • Requires more than normal setup due to his nature of turning
  • Needs to be in melee to have any cc
  • Ranged form doesn't have good resistance stats, making you quite easily killable
  • You need to synchronize well with your team with your "Mega form" to be of value

Shen

Shen is a Tank with a strong early game. But the main thing about him is his cross map ult. Giving his ally a shield
and teleporting to them.

Pros:

  • Very strong early game
  • Cross map ult, being able to save teammates or ganking a different lane.
  • Easy gank setup with his dash that taunts
  • Shield on passive, making it easy to trade favourably
  • Skill that blocks auto attacks
  • Good splitpushing lategame due to having a tp on your ult and being able to make every fight a 5v4

Cons:

  • Falls off lategame when it comes to damage
  • Requires your team to have a brain, as Shen's kit is more supportive
  • Taunt is a skillshot, if you miss you got no more movement skills
  • Badly used ult early game can completely destroy your lane

Camille

A very fun to play scaling bruiser

Pros:

  • Good early game trading with her low cd shield on her passive
  • Good in short trades
  • Very strong late game
  • Very good good lockdown with her ult
  • Long range dashes, with a stutter if you hit an enemy

Cons:

  • Dash can be blocked
  • Early game struggles, especially in longer scirmishes
  • No sustain
