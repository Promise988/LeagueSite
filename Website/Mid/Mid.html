My Top 3 Midlaners

Azir

First one coming to mind, mostly due to personal preference would be azir.

Pros:

  • One of the best late game mage in the game
  • Very fun to play
  • Always viable in any meta
  • A LOT of playmaking potential

Cons:

  • Not great early (Very mana hungry, while not doing a lot of damage)
  • Very difficult to play to perfection
  • Requires a lot of micro managing with his soldiers

Lucian

Second one, is one for the adc mains

Pros:

  • Amazingly strong early game poke
  • Scales well into late game
  • Ranged, makes it easy to deal with the many Melee midlaners
  • Dash, low cooldown if you can use spells
  • Very fun when it comes to playmaking

Cons:

  • Can get very mana hungry early game
  • Not high resistance/health stats, meaning if you don't dodge you'll get kill quite easily
  • No CC making it difficult for him to setup ganks

Zed

Last on the list and in my opinion the most fun melee character in the game. So much playmaking when you get the hang of him.

Pros:

  • Huge amount of early game damage, Wins vs majority of midlaners.
  • Very exciting to play, can always playing him differently depending on the matchup
  • Has a lot of way to outplay
  • A lot of dashing around
  • Ultimate ability makes him invinciable for the time he's ulting

Cons:

  • Very difficult to play, Not to mention when it comes to mastering
  • Requires hitting all the abilities to deal damage
  • All his abilities are skillshots and dodgable
  • Ult can be difficult, as to deal damage with it you have to hit deal damage in the low window that you have
  • Abilities have quite a high cooldown early game, so if you miss a lot early you're gonna have a difficult game
  • Doesn't scale too well, and very difficult to play when enemies get defensive items
